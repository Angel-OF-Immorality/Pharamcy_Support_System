    Our Story

    Our story in providing Pharmacy Support System began with the realization that the existing pharmacy software systems were lacking in terms of accuracy and efficiency. We noticed that pharmacists were spending a lot of time managing patient records and prescriptions, which could be better utilized in providing patient care. Patients were also facing challenges in managing their medications and keeping track of their symptoms.

    Our Mission

    We saw an opportunity to create a solution that would address these challenges and provide a more efficient and accurate way of managing patient records and prescriptions. Our Pharmacy Support System aims to streamline the process of managing patient records and prescriptions, while also providing a symptom prediction algorithm to help predict diseases based on previous symptoms.

    Our Vision

    Our vision is to provide a user-friendly interface for pharmacists, which will make pharmacists to provide better care. We believe that our Pharmacy Support System will greatly benefit the healthcare industry and improve the lives of patients.
